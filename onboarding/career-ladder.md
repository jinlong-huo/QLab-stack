# Career Ladder

Group member growth stages — expectations, privileges, and resources at each level.

## L0: Onboarding (Week 1–4)

- Complete onboarding checklist (see [welcome.md](welcome.md))
- Read 2 papers/week, produce notes using the shared template
- Attend weekly group meeting (observer)
- Set up toolchain (arXiv digest, Zotero, Python env)

## L1: Contributor (Month 2+)

- Lead 1 paper presentation/semester in group meeting
- Contribute to knowledge base (glossary, topics, reading roadmap)
- Propose and execute 1 small project (can be a repro, benchmark, or survey)
- Code review PRs from peers

## L2: Core Member (Month 6+)

- Lead 1 research project end-to-end
- Mentor L0/L1 members through onboarding and first PR
- Present at 1+ external venue (group meeting, workshop, conference)
- Own a knowledge-base topic area

## L3: Senior / Lead

- Drive group research agenda for a sub-area
- Lead paper submissions (first author or corresponding)
- External visibility (conference talks, open-source maintenance)
- Design new group infrastructure and process
